Nour packed her suitcase before sunrise.
She checked that her passport and ticket were inside.
Her brother drove her to the airport.
She passed through security without any trouble.
The plane boarded on time.
After a short flight, Nour landed in Alexandria.
She took a taxi from the airport to her hotel.
The receptionist gave her a room key.
Nour went upstairs and rested for an hour.
She suddenly woke up at home before she had packed her suitcase.
Then she called the airline to ask whether her flight would leave tomorrow.
Her brother arrived to drive her to the airport again.
